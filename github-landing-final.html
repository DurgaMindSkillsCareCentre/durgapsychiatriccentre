About D. Durga

Chennai's Most Trusted Psychologist

D. Durga is the founder of Durga Psychiatric Centre and Durga MindSkillsCare — a comprehensive mental health and soft skills platform serving patients across Chennai and India online.

Who is D. Durga?

With qualifications spanning nursing (DPN), hospital management (DAHM), marketing (BBA), human resources (MBA), and medical psychiatry social work (MSW), D. Durga brings a uniquely holistic approach to mental health care.

Based at T Nagar, Chennai, the clinic offers confidential counseling for individuals, couples, families, students, and corporates. Online sessions are available for patients across India and internationally for NRI clients.

Durga MindSkillsCare also conducts award-winning workshops on emotional intelligence, soft skills, nurse and caregiver psychology, parenting, and senior wellness — trusted by hospitals, corporates, and institutions across Tamil Nadu.
